Schools.
The school is located within one mile of the city limits of
Albuquerque, a city of 20, 000, on the main line of the Atchison, To
peka& Santa Fe railroad.
The location is convenient for the Pu
eblos and Navajos of New Mexico and Arizona.
The year has been a most successful one from every standpoint.
The enrollment was 450---- 286 boys and 164 girls. The average at
tendance was 431.
