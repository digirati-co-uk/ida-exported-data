Chapter or clan organizations are century old among the Pueblos,
and whilethey give little publicity to the purpose, membership, etc., yet
one can readily note leadership among them and strength and efficiency of
organization which perhaps accounts for primarily for commendable approach
and self- supporting status for centuries past. Their industries of what
ever line have organized direction, and collectively and individually there
is definite procedure so definite indeed that to all productive effort
within the pueblo there attaches community, moral, and religious obligation
and devotion. Their success in organization should call for our study and
understanding but of necessity taking time, patience, and sympathetic under
standing.
