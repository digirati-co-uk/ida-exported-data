- 17- 17Section V- Pield IrrigationOne irrigation project has been constructed on thisreservation near Ganado, Arizona, which is not yet completedand is under the supervision of Mr. H. F. Robinson of Albuquerque, N. M. recommendation is now before the officefor allotments to be made and with these completed the Indianswill have an opportunity for moving on the land and beginningwork under proper supervision.During the year the dam at Red Lake, thirteen milesabove the agency, washed out and so far funds have not beenavailable for replacing same. It is believed that during thefiscal year 1917 it will be possible to rebuild this dam whichis used for both irrigation and stock water but mostly for thelatter.
