PUEBLO LANDS BOARD. The Pueblo Lands Board, constituted underAct of Congress approved June 7, 1924,( 43 Stat L. 636), herebyreports under Section 2 of said Act. The Pueblo Lands Boardinvestigated and determined the exterior boundaries of thePueblos. The reports are the confirmations of the investigations and the many court cases that decided these boundaries.The collection of documents include Court papers, Right- of- way,Court Decrees and Bills of Complaint, Appraisers Reports andTestimonies.
