163Santa Fe, Now Mexico, November 10,1899.Miss Esther B. Hoyt,San 11 defonso Pueblo,Hobart, N, MexoDext' MadamI have just learned that in some of the Pueblos, there isan impression that no more pupils will be received at this school, butthis is a mistake, and I wish you would kindly inform the Indians ofyour Pueblo that I will receive as many children from San Ildefonsoas are brought here,the traveling expense runds, however, are entirely exhausted, 80 itwill be impossible for me to pay anything for their transportation,We now have over 300 pupils, but I can easily take care or a fewmore, and if there are any from your Pueblo who desire to come, I thinkat would be wise for them to come at once.Thanking you in advance, and with best wishes for your successI remean, very sinourely yours,,AtteitaSuperintendent.P.S.If any pupils wish to come, I will send our conveyence for themIin case you notify ne
