The United States Government has paid considerableattention to these Indians, to the end that perhaps 30% of them wearcivilized dress, or are accustomed to it, and an even larger percentagehave been instructed in the primary forms of education and should beable to speak and write English.The boarding school at Keams Canonhas been in operation for many years, and a large number of the Hopishave been instructed at the non- reservation schools. Few of the Hopis,however, would seem to have radically changed or departed from the oldmodes of life. As a whole, they are decidedly unmoral.
