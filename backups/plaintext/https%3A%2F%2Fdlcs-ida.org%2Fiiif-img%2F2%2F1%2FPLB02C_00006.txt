PUEBLO LANDS BOARD. The Pueblo Lands Board, constitutedunder Act of Congress approved June 7, 1924,( 43 Stat L. 636),hereby reports under Section 2 of said Act. The Pueblo LandsBoard investigated and determined the exterior boundariesof the Pueblos. The reports are the confirmations of theinvestigations and the many court cases that decided theseboundaries.
