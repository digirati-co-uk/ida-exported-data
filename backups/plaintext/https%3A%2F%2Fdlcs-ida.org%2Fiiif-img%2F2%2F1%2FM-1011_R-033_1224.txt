1930.NARRATIVE SECTION.ATLOTMENTSSeo. 5.EASTERN NAVAJO JURISDICTION.During the year there were 560 new allotments made and in anumber of cases trust patents have already been received for theallotments made during the year. The allotment work has been verymuch retarded through the transfer of special Alloting Agent,Mr. Charles E. Roblin, though there is several years work to betaken care of it all Indians are to receive the leases to whichthey are entitled under the General Allotment Act. It is the desireof the Indians and myself that he be returned to resume this dutyat the earliest possible moment.
