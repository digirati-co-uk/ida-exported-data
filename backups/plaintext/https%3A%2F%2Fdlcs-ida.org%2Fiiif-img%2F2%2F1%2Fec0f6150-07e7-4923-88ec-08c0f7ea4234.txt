SFIS Student Council
Student Council President' s address:
First and foremost I would like to congratulate the
senior class of 2007 for their accomplishments
during their high school academic years. I would
like to encourage, along with the rest of the
student council, the class of 2008 to do your best
and represent Santa Fe Indian School in a
positive manner. The council and I would like to
thank all the students of SFIS for participating in
student government and the many activities
sponsored by the student council this year.
