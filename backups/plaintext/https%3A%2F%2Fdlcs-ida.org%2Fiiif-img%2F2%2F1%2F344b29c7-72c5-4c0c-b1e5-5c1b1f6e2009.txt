ORIGINAL
Albuquerque Indian School,
July 3, 1914.
NARRATIVE.
Section I----- Law and Order.
One pupil succeeded on two occasions in purchasing liquor. It
was impossible to secure sufficient evidence to take these cases be
fore the United States Grand jury. This boy ran away and was report.
ed as a deserter. He asked later to return to the school but his re
quest was refused.
