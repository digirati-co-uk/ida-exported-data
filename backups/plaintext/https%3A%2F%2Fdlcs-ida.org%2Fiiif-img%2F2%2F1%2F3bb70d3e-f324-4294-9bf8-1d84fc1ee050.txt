August 22, 1893.Mis Annie Mae Thomas,Cubero, No MoDear madam:The Honorable Secretary of the Interior has decided tochange this school into an Indian Normal School, and I am directedto at once dispense with all the employes of the school; so youwill see that I can not give you a position here as I intendeddoing.If there is anything occurs hereafter so that I cangive you a position I will be glad to employ you and will writeto you.It is possible that you can secure a position in theNormel School.Very respectfully,InoliaSuperintendert.
