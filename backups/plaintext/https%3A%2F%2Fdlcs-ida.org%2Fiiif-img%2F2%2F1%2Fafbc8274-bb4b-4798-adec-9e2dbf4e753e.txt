A strenuous endeavor has been made to comply
with the requirements of the course of study in the
academic and industrial departments. The teacher
of agriculture has, in addition to other subjects,
taught the vocational grades in the agricultural
branches. We have generally found the sixth grade
rather hard and believe it would be wise to spend
two years on this grade. It would enable the pupils
to make a thorough review of the fifth grade work
and finish the sixth grade strong, leaving all sixth
grade pupils well prepared to enter and do the work
required in the vocational division. t.
