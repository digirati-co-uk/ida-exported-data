ANNUAL REPORT, 1925.- ZUNI AGENCY, N. M.
Section 30. 6.- Aalotments.
No allotment work has been done at this jurisdiction.
( a) It is not known when this work will be done. It
appears inadvisable to have any such work done in the near
future,
( b)) Should this Reservation be allotted there will be
no surplus lands to be thrown open to settlement. The Zuni
Indians have not to exceed half a dozen allotments on the
public domain,
