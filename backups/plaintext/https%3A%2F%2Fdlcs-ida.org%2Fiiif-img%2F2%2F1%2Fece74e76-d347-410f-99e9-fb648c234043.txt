Albuquerque School,Albuquerque, N, Mex,My Dear Sir:-Owing to the sinall pox scare, a great many of of the boysbelonging to the foot ball team have been vaccinated, and are not ableto practice, and I fear will not be able to keet your boys on noxtSaturday.I believe they will be in shape on the Saturday following, andwill then be able to play the gams.Very truly yours,a hlutSuperintendent,
