U.S. INDIAN INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL,
Santa Fe, N, M., August 1, 1892.
To the Hon, Comissioner of Indian Affairs,
Washington, D, C,
Sir:
I have the honor to inclose herewith my report of irres
ular Labor for the month of July, 1892.
With the exception of
one person, Henry J. Kendall, the names reported are pupils of
this school.
They have been empleyed whole days at represented
and have
I think, fully earned the rates and amounts reported.
I request that the report be approved,
Very respectfully
lead
Superintendent.
