Section 1----- Law and Order- Concluded.good moral lesson in language the pupils are able to understand.Prohibition has resulted in a benefit to the Indians ofthe state. There have been no cases of intoxication among thepupils and very few among the old Indians of the state. So faras is known to me, the use by Indians of peyote and mescal hasnot increased since the adoption of prohibition. I have noknowledge as to where the Indians obtain peyote and mescal.Reservation superintendents having charge of Indians who usethem will no doubt furnish information on the subject.No gambling is allowed among the pupils and so far as isknown there is no inclination on their part to gamble.There has been no legislation in the state affectingIndian affairs or Indian property rights during the year.
