1503U. S. Indian Industrial School,Santa Fe, New Mexico June 25, 1900.Mrs. Alice G. Dwire,Fueblo of Taos,Taos, New Mexico,Dear Madam:-I enclose herewith Orrigial check No. 134 for$ 216. 00, theamount due you for services as Day School Teacher at the Pueblo orTaos during the 4th quarter of 1900.Please acknowledge receipt of the same and oblige,Very respectfully,Clerk in Charge.
