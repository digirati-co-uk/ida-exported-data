NARRATIVE
Annual Report 1913
Section III- Schools.
For Tohatchi School- Cottage for principal, hospital,
and general repairs to old buildings.
For Chin Lee School- Cottages for married employees,
a hospital and general repairs.
For Cornfields Day School- Water tank, gasoline
engine and deep well pump; also an addition to the
school building for kitchen and laundry.
The corps of employees at the agency and schools
are very loyal and efficient with few exceptions. Harmony
prevails between the school and the public school authorities,
