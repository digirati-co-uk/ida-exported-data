C.Sir:I have the honor to request that I se authorized to sellone male call, about tro and one hall nonths old, and belonging tothis schooi, for not less than$ 7.00. It will not profit theschool to keep e mele call, and I recommend the sale for thatcalfreason,The cell 412 not bring more than$ 7.00, at the customary prices,Very respectfully,ImleastSuperintendent.
