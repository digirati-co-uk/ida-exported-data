Page 15.INDUSTRIESAnnual Report 1914Section IV- Industries.Some of the finest cliff dwellings and ruinsin the Southwest are found on this reservation, notablythose in the Canon De Chelly- those at Wide Ruins andthe" Red House".During May of this year seventy six boys fromthe Navajo schools were sent to work in the beat fieldsnear Garden City, Kansas. For the past two or three yearsboys have gone to the beet fields and the results obtainedwhen an escort has been sent along are most gratifying.
