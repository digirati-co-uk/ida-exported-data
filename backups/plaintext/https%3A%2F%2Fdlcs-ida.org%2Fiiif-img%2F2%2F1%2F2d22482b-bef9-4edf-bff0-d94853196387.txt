ELLEN LAWRENCEMrs. Ellen Lawrence heads the department of Pueblo Weaving, Embroidery,and Basketry. An excellent weaver and lace- maker to begin with, Mrs. Lawrencereadily learned the native weaving and embroidery of the Pueblos in her yearsamong them.Each girl in this department will take home with her a sampler covered withtrue designs. These designs are to be used by the girls and their mothers andgrandmothers in the making of garments, ceremonial dress and on other things forwhich decoration is desired.
