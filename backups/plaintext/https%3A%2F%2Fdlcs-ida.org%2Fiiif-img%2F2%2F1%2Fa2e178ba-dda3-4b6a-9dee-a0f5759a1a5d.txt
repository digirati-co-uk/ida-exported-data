VODNOLD EXCTMENTA. I. S. Alumni AssociationThe Alumni Association was organized June 9, 1920. The organizers were:Supt. Reuben Perry, Principal, Isis Harrington, Dr. Wedge and the class of 1920.The slogan adopted by this Association was “ Omaha, ” an Indian word, meaning“ up- stream ”.The Charter Membership numbered eleven. During the intervening fourteen years,the membership has grown into hundreds.
