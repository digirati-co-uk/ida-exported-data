DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR,
INDIAN SCHOOL SERVICE,
OFFICE OF SUPERINTENDENT,
Santa Fe, New Mexico, February 27,1899.
To Whom it may concern:-
The bearur- Ola Kisto- a Pima Indian girl
who has been a member of the V. S. Indian Industrial School, Santa Pe.,
N. Max., for four years is excused on account of expiration of time.
At any time she wishes to return to the school, transportation
will be furnished.
Very respectfully,
Autitet
Suporintendent.
