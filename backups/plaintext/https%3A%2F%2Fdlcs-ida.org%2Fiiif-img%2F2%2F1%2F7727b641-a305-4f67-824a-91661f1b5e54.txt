01Santa Fe, New Mexico; August 26,1899.Mr, A, H. Sousea,Laguna, Now Mexico.DearMr. sousea:,I enclose herewith$ 18, 26, which is the amount of thecheck left by you.Your ietter was received all tight, and I hop.that you are enjoying your self.I hope that you will have goodluck in securing a large number of pupils for the school.Let me know the names and ages of the pupils whom you can bringback,and I will rrange the transportation for themoHoping that you are well, and with regards to the parents ofthe Laguna children,I am very truly yours.superintendent,
