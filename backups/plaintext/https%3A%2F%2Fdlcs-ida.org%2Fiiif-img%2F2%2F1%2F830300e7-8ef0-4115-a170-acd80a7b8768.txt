Chapter or clan organizations are century old among the Pueblos,
and while they give little publicity to the purpose, membership, etc.,
yet one can readily note leadership among them and strength and efficacy
of organization which perhaps accounts primarily for commendable
approach ba self- supporting status for centuries past. Their industries
of whetever line have organized direction, and collectively and individu
ally there is definite procedure so definite indeed that to all
productive effort tithin the pueblo there attaches community, moral, and
religious obligation and devotion. Their success in organization should
call for our study and understanding but of necessity taking time,
patience, and sytopathetic understanding.
