85UNITED STATES INDIAN SERVICE,OFFICE OF SUPERINTENDENT,AgencySanta Fe, N, M., June 18, 1891.Mr. Cyrus Dixon,Pueblo of Cochiti,Now Mexico.Dear sir:I wrote to you several days ago that the funds for thisquarter had come and that I could pay you now, and thinking thatit is possible you did not get the letter I write again,I wishyou would come as soon as it is convenient.Very respectfully,SilenSuperintendent.
