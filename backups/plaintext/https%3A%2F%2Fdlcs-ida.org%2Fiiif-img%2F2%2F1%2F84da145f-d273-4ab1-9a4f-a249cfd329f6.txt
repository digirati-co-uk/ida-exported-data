U.S. Indian Industrial School,Santa Fe, New Mexico, November 14, 1903.Mrs.StarrHayes,Penasco, N.M.Madan:I have notified Mr. Sanchez to discontinue work on the well forthe present.My authority for that work has been exhausted, and I have just requested' the Department to grant additional authority to deepen the well.Whenthis is granted notice will be sent to resume work.Very respectfully,Superintendent.
