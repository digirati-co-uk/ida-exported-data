LANZAPottery DepartmentMrs. Poncho,( Acoma), InstructorLucy Garcia and Juanita Pino, Assistants in DesignEach year hundreds of pieces of beautiful pottery are made here, the instructorusing native methods and materials entirely. In the years this department has beenestablished, the school has had as its instructors some of the very best native craftsmen. They have come from San Ildefonso, Santa Clara and Acoma.Pueblo girls and boys taking Indian design at U. N. M. study each the designsof his own people, and assist the instructors in designing the ware in accordancewith the oldest of the pottery of the early Pueblo potters.
