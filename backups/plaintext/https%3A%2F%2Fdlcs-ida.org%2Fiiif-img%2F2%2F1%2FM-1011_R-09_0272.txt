THE CADET CORPS TOOK PART IN INAUGURAL PARADE.
The boys battalion of oadets took part in the in
auguration of President Woodrow Wilson on March 4, 19l 3.
They had a prominent place in the parade with the section
made up of military sohools and colleges, and their marching
was very favorably commented on by the hundreds of thousands
who viewed the parade. Six companies were utilized, march
ing with their guns, and headed by the Carlisle Indian School
band. Those who marched report a constant ovation all
along the line, and the principal newspapers and periodicals
spoke in complimentary terms of the appearance and march
ing of the students and the playing of the band. The trip
was made in a special train, the students leaving early in
the morning and re turning late the same night.
