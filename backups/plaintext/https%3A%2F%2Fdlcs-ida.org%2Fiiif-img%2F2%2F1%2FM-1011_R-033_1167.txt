ANNUAL REPORT,1929.NARRATIVE SECTION.SATESSec. 6.EASTERN NAVAJO JURISDICTION.No sales have been made. No patents in Fee issued.Nopatents in Fee for land used for Mission purposes. Heirs were determined last year in some cases of deceased Indians. IndividualIndian Moneys is limited to a few individual Indians whose allotments were leased for oil, but to date there is no commercial oildeveloped on any of them. Acreage lease and bonuses are thesource of revenue, and has been used by the individual in everycase largely to purchase sheep and goats.
