Some of the weavings done in Ms. Sunrise' s classes.
Pearl Sunrise is a Navajo who lives in
Albuquerque and has taught at U. N. M. She is
presently on the faculty at the World College
in Montezuma, N. M. She is an internationally
known artist who has traveled to Africa to
demonstrate pottery, weaving and beading.
Ms. Sunrise spent five weeks at S. F. I. S. as an
Artist- in- residence, and taught weaving to
many enthusiastic students.
