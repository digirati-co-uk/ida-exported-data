( 1)ORIGINALAlbuquerque, New MexicoJuly 1, 1916RECEIVEDJUL31916NARRATIVE.SectionI------ Law and Order.The pupils of the school have not been guilty of any infraction of the laws of the state or nation during the year. It wasreported that two boys were at one time under the influence ofliquor, but they denied it and it was impossible to obtain convincing evidence.S01The Indians of the southwest generally are a hard working,law- abiding people, who cause but little trouble. The Lagunas areanxious to have their children educated; the Acomas and Santa Anasare quite favorable to schools; the Isletas, Sandies and SanFelipes, are less favorable to schools, but many members of thesethree bands desire to have their children attend school. Wucationis growing in favor with all of these Indians, but the Lagunas willexert themselves more than any other band in order to keep theirchildren in school.
