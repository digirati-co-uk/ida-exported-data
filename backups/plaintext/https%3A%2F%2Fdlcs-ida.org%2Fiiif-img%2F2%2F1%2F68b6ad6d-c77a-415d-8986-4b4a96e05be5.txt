410INDIAN INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL,Santa Fe, New Mexico, November 23,1891.Comissioner of Indian Affairs,Washington, D.C.Sir:I have the honor to hand you herewith letter from Dr.W.S.Harroun, Physician at this school, in which he requests an increase ofsalary and ration for his horse,For reasons stated therein, I recommend that the request begranted. The school now numbers 164 pupile and the work of the Physicianis greater than it has been at any time in the past, and willprobably increase i at least not grow any 1888.Very respectfully,SucesinSuperintendent.
