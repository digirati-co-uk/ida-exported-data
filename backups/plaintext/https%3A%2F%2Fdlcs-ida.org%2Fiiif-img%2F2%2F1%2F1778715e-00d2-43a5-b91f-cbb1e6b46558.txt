Page 18.NARRATIVE.Annual Report- 1915Section V- Field Forestry.The timber on this reservation is all unallottedlands. Seventy five per cent of this is matured andshould be sola in large sales for general market and onthe stump.The best method for logging is by rail. A verysmall area is liable to fire, viz: that portion where theslashings from the saw mil1 are located. These areas arewell guarded and during the past year there were no fires.
