Annual Report

Jicar illa Indian School.
VII, Allottments:
The allottment work of this reservation was
begun in August, 1907, under act of March 1st, 1907( 34 Stat. L.,
1413), and was completed in August, 1909.
The surplus lands have not been disposed of.
