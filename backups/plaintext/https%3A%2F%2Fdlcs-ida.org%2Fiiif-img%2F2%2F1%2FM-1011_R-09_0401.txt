Section I,Law and Order, continued.I have been at this school for so short a time that I amunable to say if any effort has been made toward putting an endto the se practices, but it is my opinion that but little can bedone in this direction without the hearty cooperation of localofficers as the Indians are said to congregate in the towns onSundays, and frequently at other times, and on the se occasionsgambling and drinking are the principal pastimes.
