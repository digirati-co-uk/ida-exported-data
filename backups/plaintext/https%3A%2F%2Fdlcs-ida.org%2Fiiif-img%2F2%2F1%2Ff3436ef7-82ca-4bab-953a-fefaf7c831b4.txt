During each year a business council is called of
all prominent men and women on the reservation and all others
who wish to attend. Such subjects as better homes, child
welfare, marriage and divorce, the up- breeding of stock, the
improved method of grading and handling wool, farming, health
and sanitation, the placing in school of their children, are
all topics for discussion.
These councils are largely attended.
A session of the Indian court is held about every
sixty days to hear all complaints and for the Indian court to
pass sentence on all offenders of the law.
