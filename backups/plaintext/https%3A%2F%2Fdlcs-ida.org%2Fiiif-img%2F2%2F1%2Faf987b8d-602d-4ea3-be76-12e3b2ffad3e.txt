M.April 12, 1897.Sir:I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letterof 5th instant, in reference to the diseased condition ofsheep on the Apache Reservation in New Mexico, and to informyou the same has been referred to the Honorable the Secretaryof the Interior who has jurisdiction of this matter.Very respectfully,Secretary or war..Mr. James W. Thompson,Las Cruces,New Mexico.
