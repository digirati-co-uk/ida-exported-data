UNITED STATES INDIAN SERVICE
OFTICE OF SUPERINTENDENT,
Santa Fe, New Mexico, January, 28th, 1891
pt.Jallis,
U.S. Indian Agent,
San Carlos, Ariz.
Dear Sir:
I have your telegram of the 27th, inst.
I shall be
ready to start for your place on receiving notice from you.
I shall be glad if you can arrange transportation for me from
Alcox to an farles and return you can make such arrangement,
** 11 you kindly wire me at my expense
Cory truly yours,
Seart
Superintendent.
