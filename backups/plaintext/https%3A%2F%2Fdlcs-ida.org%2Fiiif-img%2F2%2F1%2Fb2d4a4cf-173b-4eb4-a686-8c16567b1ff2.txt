210Sr Santa Fe, New Mexico, January 2, 1900,Hon. commissioner of Indian Affairs,Washington, D.C.I have the honor to inform you that none of the window glasscelled for on my annual estimate, which was forwarded to your officeJanuary 1899, has yet been received.Our supply of glass is now entirely exhausted, and it is necessarythat more be supplied at once.I therefore respectfully request that your office notify theproper official in charge of the v., Warehouse to schedule and shipat an early date the glass estimated forVery respectfully,Your obedient servanto.Uuttasuperintendent.
