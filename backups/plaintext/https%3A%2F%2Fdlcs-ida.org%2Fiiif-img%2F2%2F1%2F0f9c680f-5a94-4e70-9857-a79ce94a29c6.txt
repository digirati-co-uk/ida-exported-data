Each year we have envied the graduating class that sat on this platform; to see them put new hopesin us. We trust that the sight of the largest class ever to graduate from thisschool will fill you with pride as it does us. We rejoice that parents, newcomers and guests have honored us in coming here and we greet you with kindwishes. Many of our comrades who would have shared gladness with us today,unfortunately are missing, some perhaps, have gone to the Great Beyond; yetwe miss them.To our instructors, teachers, and advisors, we extend our deep gratitude andappreciation for the guidance they have rendered to our success in reaching thegoal in spite of our mistakes and troubles, for they were numerous. You put usnot only in touch with the serious things of life, but also with pleasures anddifficulties we shared together.From this day on we shall enter a larger world in which obstacles, hardships, and sorrows may comfort us, but with the spirit of A. I.
