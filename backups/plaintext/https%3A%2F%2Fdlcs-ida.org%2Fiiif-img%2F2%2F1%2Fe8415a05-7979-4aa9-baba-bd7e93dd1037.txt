DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR,
UNITED STATES INDIAN INSPECTION SERVICE,
Pueblo and Jicarilla Agency, New Mexico.
Dulce, June 28th, 1900.
The Honorable.
The Secretary of the Interior.
Sir:
Enclosed please find my reports of the Pueblo and Jica
rilla Agency, New Mexico, and the Licensed Indian traders store at
Dulce, N.M. which I trust will prove satisfactory.
Very respectfully.
antuk W.Jimwar.
U.S. Indian Inspector.
