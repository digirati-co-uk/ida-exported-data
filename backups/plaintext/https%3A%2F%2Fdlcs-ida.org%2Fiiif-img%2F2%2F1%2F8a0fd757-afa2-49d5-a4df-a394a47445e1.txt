Page 16NARRATIVE.Annual report- 1915Section 1v- Industries.Some of the finest cliff dwellings and ruins of theSouthwest are found on the reservation, notably those inthe Cenon De Chelly- those at Wide Ruins and the" Red House".During May of this year seventy boys from the NavajoSchools were sent to work in the beet fields near GardenCity, Kansas. For the past thzee years boys have gone tothe beet fields and the results obtained when an escort hasbeen sent along are most gratifying.Their net earnings during the year exceeded$ 3400. 00.
