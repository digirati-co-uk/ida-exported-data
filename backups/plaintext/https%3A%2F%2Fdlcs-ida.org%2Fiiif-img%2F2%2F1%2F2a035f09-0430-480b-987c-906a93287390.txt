ANNUAL NARRATI V E R E P O R T.Jicarilla Indian Agency,Fiscal Year 1929.SECTION V.ALLOTMENTS.Alloting of these Indians was completed in 1908 under the act ofMarch 4, 1907, with timber reserved as tribal property. All surplusland has been held in common as tribal property and none of the reservation opened to homestead.No allotments have been made on the public domain.
