The divorce custom is equally simple. Dissatisfied couples
separate without ceremony. Young Indians living near the agency
are encouraged to procure license from the local authori.
ties and have the marriage ceremony performed by a minister.
The State, Federal, and Municipal Officers have shown a
willingness to cooperate in suppressing the liquor traffic among
these Indians.
It has been several years since any whiskey has
been known to be on the reservation.
The Indians under this
jurisdiction do not make" tiswin" or other harmful drinks.
The
gambling evil was discontinued several years ago after the
Indians were shown that it is a poor business to waste time and
money in gambling. Once in a while Indians will wager a small
amount on a horse race or contest of some kind, but card games
have not been played on the reservation for a number of years.
