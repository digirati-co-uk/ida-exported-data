Young Indians living near the agencyare encouraged to procure license from the local authori.ties and have the marriage ceremony performed by a minister.The State, Federal, and Municipal Officers have shown awillingness to cooperate in suppressing the liquor traffic amongthese Indians.It has been several years since any whiskey hasbeen known to be on the reservation.The Indians under thisjurisdiction do not make" tiswin" or other harmful drinks.Thegambling evil was discontinued several years ago after theIndians were shown that it is a poor business to waste time andmoney in gambling.
