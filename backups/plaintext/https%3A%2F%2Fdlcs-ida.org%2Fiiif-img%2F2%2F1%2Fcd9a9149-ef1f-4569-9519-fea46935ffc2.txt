Albuquerque ladian School.
July 3, 1914.
Section IV--- Industries.
As the industries have been discussed in Section III, nothing
remains to be said under this heading excepting that 48 of our boys are
now working in the beetfields, two in the Santa Fe Shops, and eight em
ployed as carpenters on the buildings being erected at this school.
Seven girls are working for families in town.
