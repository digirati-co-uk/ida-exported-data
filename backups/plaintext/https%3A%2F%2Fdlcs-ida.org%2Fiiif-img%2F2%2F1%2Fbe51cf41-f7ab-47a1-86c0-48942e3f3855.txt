NEW MEXICO.LAND DIVISION- POPULATION SECTION.The Navajos are a self supporting race and receive nothing fromthe Government in the way of annuities or shares of trust funds.Their income is derived from the raising of cattle and sheep, especiallythe latter, and blankets woven by the women.As a tribe they have the reputation, among traders and othershaving business dealings with them, of being very close in a tradeand thrifty and careful of what little money they have.
