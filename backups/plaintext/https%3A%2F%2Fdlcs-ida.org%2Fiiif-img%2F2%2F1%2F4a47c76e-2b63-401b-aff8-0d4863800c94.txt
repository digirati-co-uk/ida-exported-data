Albuquerque Training School.Albuquerque, New Mexico.June 30, 1922.NARRATIVESECTION1.......... LAW AND ORDER.The conduct of the pupils has been uniformly goodduring the entire year. They have shown a splendid spiritof cooperation and have seemed anxious to advance in theiracademic studies as well as in the industrial and domestictraining.They have manifested a deep interest in thewelfare of the institution and a desire to see it enlarged,improved and better equipped. There have been nine teendesertions during the year. Of these eighteen were returned.With the exception of the se few cases of runaways but littlecriticism can be offered on the conduct and behavior of thestudent body.The Catholic Sisters instruct the pupils of that faithat the school on Saturdays in the afternoons and on Sunday
