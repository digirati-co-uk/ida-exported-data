Santa Po, N.M
April 27,1897.
Hoco Commissioner of Indian Artairs,
Vashington, D. C.
I have the honor to stat. that I have hauled all the manure
I have made, and now need 75 loads to finish manuring the fruit tries,
grass and riowors, and vegetable ground.
I ask authority to purchase these loads at not to exceed 50 cents
por load, for immediate us.
Very respaorfully,
Mother
Jones
Superintendent.
