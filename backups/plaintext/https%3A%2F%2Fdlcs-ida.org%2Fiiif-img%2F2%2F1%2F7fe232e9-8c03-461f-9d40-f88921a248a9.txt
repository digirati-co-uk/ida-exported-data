U. S, INDIAN INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL,
Santa Fe, N, M., July 23, 1892.
To the Hon, Commissioner
of Indian Affairs,
Washington, D, C.
Sir:
I have the honor to request that I be authorized to expend
$ 6.00 per quarter during the fiscal year ending June 30th, 1993,
for postage stamps, for use of pupils in school in correspondence
with parents and guardians;- total for the year$ 24,00.
I have
made requisition for funds for miscellaneous expenditures during
the 1st quarter, 1893.
Very respectfully
What
Superintendent.
