Miss Marie JensenMr. John Wesley ThompsonDEDICATIONWe, the Class of 1953, dedicate this Annual to Miss Marie Jensen and Nir. JohnWesley Thompson, Indian Service employees, as a token of gratitude for their long,faithful service.Miss Jensen, daughter of immigrant parents, educated herself, receiving herMaster of Arts degree from the University of California. She has traveled abroad insix different countries. Miss Jensen has written a novel," Just Beyond," a booklet ofpoems, a play," Will O' the Wisp," and her poems have appeared in two anthologies.Mr. Thompson, long a resident of New Mexico, served in the First World War.His good citizenship has been an inspiration to us all.Miss Jensen and Mr. Thompson, your example has been an inspiration to themany Indian students who have attended Santa Fe Indian School.
