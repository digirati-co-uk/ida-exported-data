REEDluRIGINALAUG 14 197Annual Report,Santa Fe School,1917.SECTION II.H_E_A_L_T_HThe health of the pupils during the fiscalyear ending June 30, 1917, WES excellent. Therewere no epidemics of any kind and no cases ofpneumonia.The" Save the Baby" campaign was continuedduring the past year by instruction in the classrooms, talks by the physician, nurse and metrons,so that this important subject was deeply impressed upon the minds of the Indian pupils.
