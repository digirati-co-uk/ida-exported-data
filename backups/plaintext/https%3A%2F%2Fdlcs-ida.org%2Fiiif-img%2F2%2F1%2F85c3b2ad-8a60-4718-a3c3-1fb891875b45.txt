When in Tuba City July 29th, 1898, to interrogate theMormons residing there regarding the Moencopie Indian allotments, 1administered the usual form of oath to all of them who were present,among whom was D. Brinkerhort, a Bishop of the Mormon Church for theTuba City District. He did not give me any statement at the time,but said to me that should he think of anything bearing upon thematter he would write it out and hand it to me.He did not haveany statement written out when I loft Tuba City, but I received thestatement in affidavit form, as above shown, through the mail atFlagstaff, Arizona, on August 3rd, 1898,hannsS. Indian Inspector.
