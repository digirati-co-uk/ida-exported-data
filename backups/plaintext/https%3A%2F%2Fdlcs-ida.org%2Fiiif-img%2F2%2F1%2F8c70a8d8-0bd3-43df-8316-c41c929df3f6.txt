NARRATIVE SECTION.Annual Report, 1919,Section VI.San Juan SchoolAllotments.No allotments have been made on this reservation. Therehave been one or two families of Indians whohave made inquiries about allotments on the publio domain of Utah butthey have not as yet furnished the information called forand not much progress has been made.No reservation allotments should be made on the reservation for many years to come.No equitable or just distribution of the land is possible in this way.Common useof the range is the only and equitable and just plan.28.
