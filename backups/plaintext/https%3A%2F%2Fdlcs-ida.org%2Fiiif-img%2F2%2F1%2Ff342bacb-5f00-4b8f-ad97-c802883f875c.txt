Santa Fe, N. M., Nov. 2nd, 1903.LymanJ. Maxwell,AdditionalFarmer,.Thornton, N. M.DearSir:I am sorry to hear that you have been siok, and hope you will soonbe out again. You should send in a report for the month of Oct. showing the number of days you were on duty in the month etc. You know thatwe are expected to make monthly school reports during vacation, and Ipresume you should send in a report if you were not on duty any duringthe month.Respectfully,SeSuperintendent.
