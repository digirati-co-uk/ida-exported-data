Thornton, N. M.
Dear
Sir:
I am sorry to hear that you have been siok, and hope you will soon
be out again. You should send in a report for the month of Oct. show
ing the number of days you were on duty in the month etc. You know that
we are expected to make monthly school reports during vacation, and I
presume you should send in a report if you were not on duty any during
the month.
Respectfully,
Se
Superintendent.
