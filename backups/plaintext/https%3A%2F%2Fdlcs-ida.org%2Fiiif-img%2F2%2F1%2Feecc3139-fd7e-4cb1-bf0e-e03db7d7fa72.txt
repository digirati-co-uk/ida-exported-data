In reply to:
DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR,
INDIAN SCHOOL SERVICE,
OFFICE OF SUPERINTENDENT,
Santa Fe, N. Mo, March 28, 1893,
The Hon. Com of Indian Affairs,
Washington, D. C.
I have the honor to request that I be authorised to sell
one male calf, about four weeks old, belonging to the school, for
not less than$ 8.00,
for the reason that it is not profitable
to keep male calves here.
Very rospectfully
,
Hulin
Superintendent.
