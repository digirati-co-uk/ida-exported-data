ANNUAL REPORT,1929.NARRATIVESECTIONAITOLOENIS,Seo. 5.EASTERN NAVAJO JURISDICTION.During the year Special Allotting Agent, Mr. Charles E. Roblin,has made 212 new allotments, 457 old applications still pending.Many in the past have been rejected, but the total in good standingnumbers 2,334. Trust patents for the majority have issued tho todate no patents in Fee. There are more than five years' allotmentwork at this jurisdiction alone, and perhaps a like amount of workunder the Southern Navajo Jurisdiction. This work is difficult, asthere is so much examination in the field to be made preliminary tothe filing of applications.
