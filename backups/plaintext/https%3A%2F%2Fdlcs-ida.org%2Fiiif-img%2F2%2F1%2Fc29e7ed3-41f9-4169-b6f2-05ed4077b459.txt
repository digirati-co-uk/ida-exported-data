Section IX---- Sales
Annual Report
1911
Jicarilla Indian School.
IX. Sales:
Patents in fee have not been issued to any of
the Indians on thit reservation.
It will be a long time before the restrictions
can be removed on this reservation, as these Indians are wholly
incompetent.
There has been no sales of inherited or surplus
lands.
Luhansia!
Supt.& Spel. Disb. Agent,
