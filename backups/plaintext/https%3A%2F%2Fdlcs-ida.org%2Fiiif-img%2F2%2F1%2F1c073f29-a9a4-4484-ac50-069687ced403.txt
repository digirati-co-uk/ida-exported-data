Page 5NARRATIVE.Annual report 1915.Section 1------ Law and order.During the past year there has been some gamblingbut these offenderswere promptly dealt with by the courtof Indianoffenses.In general the liquor situation is better than lastyear but there is constant need for vigilance by everyemployee. The head men of the Tribe are cooperating withthe Superintendent in this regard.Complete supervisionover traders on this reservationis had and they have very generally followed out regulations.
