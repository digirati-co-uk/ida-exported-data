346U.S. INDIAN INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL,Santa Fe, N. Mo, July29, 1892.To the Hon. Cornmissioner of Indian Affairs,Washington, D. C.Sir:I have the honor to report that in making the annual estimate for the present year, coal was omitted.I desire authorityeither to purchase in open market or to advertise for proposals.The school will require 150 tons,The market price is$ 5.50 perton in car load lots, and$ 6,00 in smaller lots.If time istaken to advertise for proposals, it will be necessary to purchase a few tons( not to exceed 10 tons) for immediate use.I desire early instructions in the matter,Very respectfullyAle AliastSuperintendent.
