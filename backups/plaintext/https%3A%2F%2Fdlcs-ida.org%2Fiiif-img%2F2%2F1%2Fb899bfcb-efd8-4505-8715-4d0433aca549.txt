Section VI- Fiela- Irrigation
Annual Report 1913
But very little has been done in the way of
irrigation on this reservation. A few dry reservoirs and
ditches were built some few years ago which have been of
no use to the Indians. The only method of securing water
is by floods and melting snow. Large wells might be sunk
to advantage and large pumps installed for irrigation at
some points.
