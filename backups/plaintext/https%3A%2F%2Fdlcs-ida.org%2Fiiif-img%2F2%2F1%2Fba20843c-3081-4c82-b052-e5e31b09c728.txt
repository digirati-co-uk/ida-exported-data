109Santa Pe, New Mexico, August 30, 1899.Dear Robert;I enclose herewith six vouchers for your signature, whichplease sign on the line marked with pencil, and return them as soonas possible,The amount dus you is$ 10. 10, and it will be held until you return.I hope you are having a good time, and will be successfulin finding a number of pupils who would like to come to this school.Hoping to hear from you soon, and with kind regards,I remain very truly yours,aduzSuperintendente
