Theseclasses are the Pottery Making, Weaving, Silversmithing, Pueblo Embroidery and IndianArt. Mrs. Caroline Poncho, an Acoma, has eight pupils in her Pottery Making classes,Mrs. Elizabeth Burnsides, Navajo, has 25 pupils in her Weaving classes. Mr. Hoska Burnsides, Navajo, has eight pupils in his Silversmithing classes. Mrs. Ellen Lawrence has32 pupils in her Pueblo Embroidery classes. Miss Louise F. Prentice has61 pupils in her Indian Art classes. This department is attempting to correlate good Indian design with fine craftsmanship. Pottery and Weaving have been taught here in thepast and excellent work has been done under native instructors. Interest in Indian designand painting was started with a small group who studied at the university during the year1930- 31.
