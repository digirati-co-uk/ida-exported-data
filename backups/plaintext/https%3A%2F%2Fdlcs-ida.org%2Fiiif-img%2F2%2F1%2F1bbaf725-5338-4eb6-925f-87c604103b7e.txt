U.S. Indian Industrial School,
Santa Fe, New Mexico, October 24, 1903.
Miss Emma Dawson,
Jemez, N.M,
Madam
:
I enclose herewith a copy of a letter received from the owners
of the buildings ocoupied by the Jemez Day School,
You may proceed to have two water- closets erected and and other necess
ary repairs made. The total cost of sare should not exceed$ 50.00, and
when such improvements and repairs are completed, forward bills for same
to me.
Very respectfully,
Vannuudl
TWV:
Superintendent.
