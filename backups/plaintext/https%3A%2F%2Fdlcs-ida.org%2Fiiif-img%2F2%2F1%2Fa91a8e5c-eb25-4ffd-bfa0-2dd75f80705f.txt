Pueblo Weaving and EmbroiderySPINNING WHEELS AND LOOMMrs. Ellen Lawrence, InstructorDorothy Mahkewa and Rose Pavatea, Assistants( Pueblo boys studying Art in U. N. M. assist with designs and weaves.)Here the ancient weaving and embroidery of the pueblos is carried on. Pueblogirls who wish may learn the half forgotten weaving of their grandmothers, carrying back to their homes, not only the patterns and articles made, but the spirit oftheir ancient craft. Basketry will be added to this department as soon as possible.
