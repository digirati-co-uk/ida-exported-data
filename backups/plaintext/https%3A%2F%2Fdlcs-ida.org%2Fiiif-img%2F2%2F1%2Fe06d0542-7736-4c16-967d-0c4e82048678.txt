Chapter or clan organizations are century old among the Pueblos, and
While they give little publicity to the purpose, membership, etc.,
yet one can readily note leadership among them and strength and
efficacy of organization which perhaps accounts primarily for commend
able approach and self- supporting status for centuries past.
Their Industries of whatever line have organized direction, and
collectively and individually there is definite procedure, 80 definite
indeed that to all productive effort within the pueblo there attaches
Community, moral and religious obligation and devotion. Their success
in organization should call for our study and understanding but of
necessity taking time, patience and sympathetic understanding.
