269INDIAN INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL,Santa Fe, N. M., Sept. 16, 1891.Commissioner of Indian Affairs,Washington, D. 0.Sir:I have the honor to request that I be authorized to purchase,from Prinds in hand, a small range or cooking stove for the amployea" mess, at nor to exceed$ 55,00 including furniture,The school is increasing in numbers and it is not convenient tocook for the employes on the saras stove.Very respectfully,eartSuperintendent.
