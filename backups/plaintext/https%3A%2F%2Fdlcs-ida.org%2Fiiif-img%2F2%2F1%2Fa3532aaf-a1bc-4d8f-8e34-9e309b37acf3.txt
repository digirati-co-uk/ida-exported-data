SCHOOL- Continued.WaAn encouraging feature in the education of the Indianyouth of the southwest is found in the fact that largernumbers than formerly was the case remain in school tofinish higher grades and to graduate. The sentiment is growing in favor of more advanced education. A majority of themembers of the class of 1922 will enter Haskell Institute orthe local high school to finish the Junior and senior yearsof high school.
