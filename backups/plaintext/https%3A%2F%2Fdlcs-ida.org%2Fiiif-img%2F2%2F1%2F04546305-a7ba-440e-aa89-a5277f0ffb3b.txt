414INDIAN INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL,Santa Fe, N. M., November 24, 1891.Commissionerof Indian Affairs,Washington, D, C.gir:In answer to letter from your office under date of botober14th last,regarding my cash and property accounts for the 2ndquarter 1891, I have to say that the 31 pounds of Beef st111 sharedto me was expended for subsistence of pupils in school during the3rd quarter 1892, as shown by the weekly issues for that quarter.This reply would have been made promptly, but for the factthat I expected to receive a statement of exceptions to my accountsfor the third quarter soon, and intended answering this exception incanneshowwith the answers to 3rd quarter's exceptione.Very resepatfully,Superintendona.
