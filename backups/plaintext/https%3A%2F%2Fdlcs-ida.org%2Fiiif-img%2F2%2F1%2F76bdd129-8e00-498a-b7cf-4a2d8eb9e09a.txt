Venereal disease is not prevalent among these pueblos, isolated
cases only being found.
Estimate of Jemez, Sia, Santa Ana, Sandia and San Felipe is five
active cases.
This does not include hereditary syphilis( syphilitio
taint). If these latter were included probably fifty would be more
nearly correct.
All cases that will accept treatment are being treated.
Such cases as have refused treatment are being urged to accept it.
The only aggressive campaign against venereal disease possible would
be the hospitalization of selected cases and they could be taken care
of both at Laguna Sanatorium and the trachoma hospital in Albuquerque,
which could gradually be turned to more general usage as trachoma
becomes eradicated.
